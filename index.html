What's This About?

In October 2024, Manchester United fan Frank Ilett (@TheUnitedStrand) made a promise: he wouldn't cut his hair until the Red Devils won 5 games in a row.

Months later, he's still waiting. The challenge has become a viral sensation, with thousands of fans following his journey on social media.
